Overview

Channel is the object in charge of receiving the messages from a sender and deliver them to the receivers. They can be delivered synchronously ( sent immediately) or asynchronousley (they are stored in the messages collection)

Instance Variables: listeFonctionObs<(Collection of: FonctionObs)>In Cormas there is an interface to observe the exchanges of messages. Instances of object FonctionObs is in charge of establishing a linkage between the Chaneel and the interface . messages<(Collection of: (LensAbsentee | Object))>a collection of messages population<LensAbsentee | Object>the population of agents registered. Automatically any instace of AgentComm, GroupComm, AgentCommLocation and GroupCommLocation is registered.
