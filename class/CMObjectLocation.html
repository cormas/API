Overview

An instance of this class or its subclass should be a simple located entity of the world. It is situated on a spatialEntityElement called its 'patch'.

Instance Variables: patch the location of the passive object as a reference to a spatial entity. Symetrically, the spatial entity records the objects located there in its "theOccupants" attribute, a dictionary of collections of located entities. center comment
